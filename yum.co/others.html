Privacy Policy

Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit our website . Our website address is: yum.co

What rights you have over your data

If you have an account on this site, or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

How long we retain your data

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This is so we can recognise and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue.For users that register on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (except they cannot change their username). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

Return Policy

Our Return Policy

1. Items must be returned within 20 minutes of delivery.
2. The return label should be scanned by the carrier within half an hour of delivery.
3. Packaging must not be damaged in any way and only the original packaging.
4. A 20% fee shall be applied to the items returned. Original shipping and handling fee are non-refundable.
5. Your product refund will be issued to the payment method used to make the original purchase.
6. Failure to follow these conditions will result in a reduced or no refund.

Return Procedure

1. Log in to your account.
2. Select the item you wish to return.
3. We will email you a return label to send the item bac to us.
4. Include a copy of your order.
5. Give the packages to the carries.
6. For the sake of food being a perishable product, time must be considered as stated in the policy above.
